Flash Card Generator is a dynamic study application. Creating a user profile allows you to store two types of flash cards. One is a basic card that has a question on one side and an answer on the other. You can also create "cloze" flash cards. These are fill in the blank cards with one side containing the full written answer and the other side containing the same information but excluding a portoin of the answer of your choosing. The application uses Firebase to store your created cards and grant you access to them based on your user profile.
